Ahead of time:
- Make secret sauce

For guacamole:
- Peel avocados
- Cut tomatoes in quarters
- Chop white and light green parts of green onions
- Mince hot pepper
- Mash tomatoes, hot pepper, green onions, and lime juice with avocado
- Season with salt and pepper
- Splash with drops of secret sauce
- Sprinkle with feta

Serve with tortilla chips or eat on tacos, burritos, huevos rancheros, etc.
